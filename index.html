About Damiano

Damiano was born on July 31st, 1998, in Rovigo, Italy. He began playing flute at the age of nine. His formal musical education started in 2009 at his local muisc school and continued, from 2012 to 2019, at the "Franceso Venezze" Conservatory of Music in Rovigo under M° Gabriella Melli.


In the last year of his Bachelor's degree (2018-2019) he studied at the Utrechts Conservatory (the Netherlands) as an Erasmus student, under the guidance of M° Aldo Baerten (Antwerp Symphony Orchestra principal flutist). In 2019 Damiano graduated from the Rovigo Conservatory with 110 cum laude and merit.


In 2019 he started his Master's degree at the Royal Conservatory of Antwerp, Belgium (still studying under M° Aldo Baerten), where he graduated with Magna cum laude in June 2022.


During his studies Damiano participated in several competitions, where he was awarded, among others, Second Prize at the 29th edition of the Città di Moncalieri Competition 2017 (Turin), Third Prize at the “Crescendo” competition 2017 in Padua and, more recently, Third Price at the International Music Competition "Città di Cervignano"; he also took part in Masterclasses held by M° Francesco Loi, M° Paolo Taballione, M° Filippo Mazzoli and M° Gaby Pas-Van Riet.


During his studies he cultivated a great passion for chamber music. In 2018-2019 he performed regularly with the wind quintet “Epos Ensemble” and other ensembles in important halls in the Netherlands (Kleine Zaal and Spiegelzaal at the Concertgebouw, Amsterdam; in the latter he also performed for the radio broadcast NPO Klassiek).

He is currently based in Zaanstad, the Netherlands, where he regularly performs with local professional musicians.
